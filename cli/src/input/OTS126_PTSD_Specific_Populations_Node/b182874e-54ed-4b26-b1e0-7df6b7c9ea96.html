The global lifetime prevalence of PTSD is 4-10%12.

PTSD is one of the most common mental disorders in the US, with 7-8 out of every 100 people experiencing PTSD at some point in their lifetime34.

~13 million adults in the US will experience PTSD during a given year5.

>80% of PTSD patients are in the general population rather than the military population6.

The typical age at which PTSD first appears is during young and middle adulthood. Among adults in the United States, the median age of onset if around 23 years old7.
