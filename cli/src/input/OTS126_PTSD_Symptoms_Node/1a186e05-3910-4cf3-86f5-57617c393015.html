Avoidance of or efforts to avoid distressing memories, thoughts, or feelings [4]

Research consistently shows a link between avoidant coping and the severity of PTSD symptoms. Avoidance impedes the processing of traumatic memories and the reduction of associated negative emotions, hindering natural recovery and reinforcing the perception of danger associated with those memories [15]. Additionally, studies suggest that females tend to exhibit more pronounced avoidance, highlighting gender differences in PTSD symptomatology [16].
