Reckless or self-destructive behavior

The severity of PTSD correlates with challenges in impulse control, often leading to impulsive, reckless, and potentially self-destructive behaviors [28] [29]. These actions can range from substance abuse and risky sexual behaviors to intentional self-harm [30] [31]. A study on Veterans receiving treatment for PTSD revealed that a significant number of deaths were related to substance use, suicide, and homicide [32].
