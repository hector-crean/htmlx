The 36-Item Short-Form Health Survey (SF-36), is a self-report for evaluating health-related quality of life (HRQoL).

Format: a 36-item questionnaire that measures across 8 domains.

  1. physical functioning

  2. role physical

  3. bodily pain

  4. general health

  5. vitality

  6. social functioning

  7. role emotional

  8. mental health
