Cardiometabolic disorders

Studies have shown that PTSD is linked to higher rates of physical health issues related to immune dysregulation, including metabolic syndromes, diabetes, atherosclerotic cardiovascular disease, and autoimmune diseases23. Additionally, individuals with PTSD have an increased risk of hypertension, hyperlipidemia, obesity, and cardiovascular disease24. Specifically, research on female survivors of the 9/11 attacks with PTSD indicates a heightened risk of hospitalization due to heart disease25.
