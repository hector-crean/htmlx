Brain regions involved in PTSD

  • Hippocampus: involved in memory consolidation and maintenance of long-term memory [1]

  • Prefrontal Cortex: involved in v, thinking, cognitive processes, and attention [1]

  • Amygdala: involved in emotional and behavioral regulation, regulates fear [1]

  • Insula: responsible for sensory processing, emotions, and motivation [2].

  • Anterior cingulate cortex: involved in decision-making and impulse control [1]
